CERN(European Council for Nuclear Research)

CERN is the European Organization for Nuclear Research, a leading global center for particle physics research, home to the world’s largest particle accelerator, the Large Hadron Collider.


CERN, established in 1954, is an intergovernmental organization headquartered near Geneva, Switzerland, on the Franco-Swiss border, with 25 member states and Israel as a full member outside Europe


Its primary mission is to explore the fundamental structure of matter and the universe by providing advanced particle accelerator facilities and infrastructure for high-energy physics research
